This micro-small project is to showcase the properties of float and clear.
The float is applied to the image to float on the left and the paragraph is wrapped around it.
The footer has been spared from wrapping the image by using the associated property called: clear.
The clear: left has made the footer to get clear of wrapping and instead is placed in a separate line.
